Predicting Miami Housing Prices using Bayesian Machine Learning

To gain insights into the real estate market, understand trends in property values, and assess affordability given the impact of economic factors like inflation, interest rates, and changing work. I employed Bayesian methods to clearly quantify and describe the uncertainty around predictions for potential homebuyers, communicating a range of expected sales prices instead of a single estimate.

This approach helps buyers better prepare for market dynamics and make more informed decisions.
